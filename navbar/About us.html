About Us

Lasting Strength Through Jewelry

Our core mission has always been to design jewelry that is not only beautiful but also imparts a sense of strength and endurance. Our jewelry is crafted to be more than just accessories; they are tools that help you feel like the very best version of yourself every day. With care for both you and our planet, our mission is to harmonize beauty and sustainability in every piece of jewelry we create, and we look forward to continuing this work well into the future.

Elevating Style

We have focused on creating everyday jewelry that truly makes you feel fantastic. Since our company was founded in the year 2000, our primary goal has been to become the most responsible and sustainable version of ourselves. This includes to use 100% recycled gold and sterling silver in our jewelry.

The Owner

Marta Andersson started her own jewelry business in 2012. In the beginning, it was a small store mostly for friends and family, but now people all over the world know about it. In the early days, she created jewelry that reminded her of her hometown and her upbringing there. Later, as she traveled around Europe and the USA, her jewelry was influenced by the different places she visited. Marta wants her jewelry to be both beautiful and not too expensive. She wants everyone to be able to afford them, and that's a big reason why her business has become so successful.
